My Tuning Hobby

Tuning has always been a small hobby of mine. I work mostly on large automobile engines, but I've spent some time working with small engines in lawnmowers and gasoline powered tools as well. Optimizing a naturally aspirated engine is something I've always found very satisfying. My next step in tuning will be to work with forced induction, but I am a big believer in displacement over boost. Working on a larger engine with no supercharger/turbocharger is much easier, cheaper, and hassle free for a novice tuner like myself. A turbocharger could possibly save me gas money, but the kits are always too expensive to make it worthwhile I've found. So, my most likely choice will be to work with a supercharger for the sake of interest and fun.
